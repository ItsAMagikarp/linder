About Linder

Our Creators

Linder is developed by: Brian Tran, Isaac Phoon Nicholas, Stanley Gian, and Triet Phan.

We created Linder to give a more efficient method of application to employment seekers. We also seek to give employers the opportunity to find available applicants through our site. With Linder we aim to make employment simpler, faster, and organized.

Represent yourself and find the right fit

With profile creation and updating, you can show off your skills and experience to attract job offers. As employers, you can fully display your company and find the perfect applicants.

Distinguish yourself with tags

With the tag system and filter tags, you can easily find positions that fit your criteria in a matter of seconds. Employers can go through applications faster than ever by filtering in/out tags.

Stay up to date with your industry!

From chat logs to video calls, Linder is full of ways to connect employers with applicants and keep you up to date with the latest news in your industry

Join and find the perfect job for you today with Linder.
